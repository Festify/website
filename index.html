How Festify works

  1. You create a new Party and get a party code

  2. Your guests enter the party code in their mobile browser

  3. Everyone votes to decide which tracks should be played

Features

  • Super easy Setup

    Creating a Party using Festify couldn't be simpler. Just download the App, log in with your Spotify Premium Account and have fun.

  • Spotify-Powered

    Festify uses Spotify's huge music library so your guests can choose from millions of tracks. An internet connection and a Spotify Premium account are all you need to get your next Party started.

  • Democratic Voting

    Your guests can vote for songs using their smartphones. The more people vote for a track, the higher it moves up your queue. Using this system, Festify will only play the music that your guests like.

  • Easy to join

    To participate in your Festify Party, your guests just scan the displayed QR Code or go to fstfy.de and enter your Party Code. No need to download any additional apps – everything is handled online. The Voting System works on any device – try it yourself!

  • Fallback Playlists

    Sometimes, your guests are too lazy to add new tracks to your party queue, but we've got you covered. When you create a Festify Party, you can add a Fallback Playlist from your Spotify Account that will be played if no other songs are queued.

  • The Looks

    Festify is designed to look good on your computer, notebook, TV, or projector. Fan art of each playing artist from Fanart.TV automatically appears in the background. If no fan art is available, the player shows the current album cover.

  • Admin Mode

    As the party host, you can control Festify using your Smartphone using the party's Admin Password. This includes skipping and deleting tracks from the queue and pausing the player if needed.

  • More to come!

    We're still working on new features for Festify. We're planning to add Blacklists, and more in the future. In the meantime, if you find any bugs or have new ideas for Festify, feel free to contact us.
